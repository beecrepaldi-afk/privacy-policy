VerbaDiem Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 25, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how the VerbaDiem application ("we," "us," or "our"), developed by Beatriz Crepaldi, handles your information. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

VerbaDiem is designed to respect your privacy. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable data, such as your name, email address, or location.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

For the app to function correctly and save your progress, some information is stored directly on your device using the browser's local storage. This data includes:

  • Your learning progress (learned words, XP, level).
  • Your current and longest streak.
  • Unlocked achievements.
  • Your language settings.
  • Word collections you have created.

This data remains exclusively on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third-party servers under our control.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide all app features, VerbaDiem utilizes the following third-party services. Each service has its own privacy policies regarding how they handle data.

3.1. Google Gemini API

The core of the VerbaDiem learning experience is powered by Google's Gemini API. To generate daily words, etymologies, example sentences, and conduct guided practice sessions, the app sends certain non-personal information to Google. This includes:

  • The native and target language preferences you have selected.
  • The daily word being learned.
  • Sentences you type during practice sessions for evaluation.

This information is processed by Google to provide the AI's responses. We recommend you review the Google Privacy Policy to understand how they manage data.

3.2. Google AdSense

To support the development of the app, we display advertisements provided by Google AdSense. AdSense may use cookies and device identifiers to collect non-personal data and serve ads more relevant to you.

For more information on how Google uses data in its advertising products, please visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

4. App Permissions

The application does not request access to your camera, microphone, location (GPS), contacts, or any other sensitive device permissions.

5. Children's Privacy

VerbaDiem is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can take necessary action.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer at: beecrepaldi@gmail.com.
